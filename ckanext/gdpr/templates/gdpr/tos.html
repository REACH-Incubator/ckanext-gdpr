    Background and introduction

    REACH Data Catalogue is part of the EuRopEAn incubator for trusted and secure data value CHains (REACH) project, funded by the European Union’s Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme under grant agreement No 951981.

    The REACH Incubator is a Data Value Chain (DVC) incubator offering a dedicated acceleration programme and up to €100k equity free for EU based startups and teams. REACH defies DVC innovators to sort out data -based challenges for a range of EU organizations in a myriad of sectors, or to propose their own concepts/ideas making use of our Data Catalogue.
